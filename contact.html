FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to set up the TMS?

expand_more

Our team can typically onboard your fleet and configure the TMS within 2–4 weeks, depending on fleet size and integrations required. We provide dedicated onboarding managers to ensure a smooth transition.

Do you offer a free trial or demo?

expand_more

Yes! We offer a free 30-minute live demo where our product specialist walks you through every module. No credit card required to get started.

What industries does Logibrisk serve?

expand_more

We serve 3PL providers, cold chain, broker dispatch, e-commerce, construction, manufacturing, taxi & mobility, express cargo, and full/part truckload transport industries.

Is my data secure with Logibrisk?

expand_more

Absolutely. We use enterprise-grade encryption (AES-256), regular security audits, and comply with data protection regulations. Your data is hosted on secure cloud infrastructure with 99.9% uptime SLA.

Can Logibrisk integrate with my existing systems?

expand_more

Yes. Logibrisk supports 50+ GPS hardware providers and offers REST APIs for seamless integration with ERPs (SAP, Tally), e-commerce platforms (Shopify, WooCommerce), and custom applications.
